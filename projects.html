🏎️ Kart Physics Prototype

This prototype focuses on vehicle movement and physics-based gameplay developed in Unreal Engine 5 using Blueprint scripting. The project features a drivable kart with custom movement mechanics, steering controls, acceleration, braking, and drifting behavior. The objective was to explore vehicle physics, player control responsiveness, and arcade-style driving mechanics while creating a smooth and enjoyable driving experience.

🎮 Gameplay Mechanics Showcase

This gameplay mechanics prototype was developed independently in Unreal Engine 5 using Blueprint scripting. The project demonstrates a variety of interactive gameplay systems including wearable equipment, door interactions, hazard detection, football scoring mechanics, player death zones, and respawn functionality. The objective was to explore different gameplay programming concepts and create reusable systems commonly found in modern games.

🎮 Gameplay Systems Prototype

This gameplay systems prototype was developed independently in Unreal Engine 5 using Blueprint scripting. The project focuses on core gameplay mechanics that form the foundation of many games, including health management, damage calculation, healing functionality, checkpoint saving, player death detection, and respawn systems. The objective was to create a reliable and reusable gameplay framework while gaining experience with event-driven gameplay logic and player state management.
