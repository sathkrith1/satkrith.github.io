☠️ Survival Horror

AI · Stealth · Atmosphere · Unreal Engine 5

📄 About This Project

This survival horror prototype was developed in Unreal Engine 5 and focuses on creating tension through AI-driven gameplay and environmental awareness. The project features a custom enemy AI capable of detecting the player through sight and sound using Unreal Engine's NavMesh navigation system. All character animations were created and integrated using Animation Blueprints, resulting in responsive movement, smooth transitions, and immersive horror gameplay centered around stealth and survival.
